What is a “qualified sales call”

A qualified sales call is booked with a prospect who matches your ideal customer profile, has shown interest in your offering, and is ready to discuss their needs.

What if a lead isn’t qualified?

We focus on reaching prospects who align with your ideal customer profile and have expressed genuine interest in your offering. Every lead is pre-screened and approved by you, ensuring only high-quality leads make it to your calendar.

How soon can I expect results?

Most clients see their first booked calls within 1st week of launching a campaign. The timeline depends on factors like your target audience, campaign specifics and your product market fit.

What happens if a lead doesn’t show up for a call?

We confirm all appointments and minimize no-shows with follow-ups, but if a lead doesn’t show, we work with you to re-engage them.

How does your pricing work?

We operate on a pay-per-call model, so you only pay for the qualified calls we deliver.

Do you work with all industries?

We specialize in B2B businesses and tailor our campaigns to fit your specific niche.

What kind of support will I receive during my campaign?

You’ll be assigned a dedicated campaign strategist who will guide you through onboarding, provide ongoing 1-on-1 support, share real-time updates via Slack or WhatsApp, and address any questions throughout the campaign.
